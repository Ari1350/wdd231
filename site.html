Bolivian Flowers - Website Plan

Site Name

The site name, Bolivian Flowers, was chosen to represent the diverse and vibrant flora native to Bolivia. The name is simple, descriptive, and easily memorable for users interested in Bolivian culture and botany.

Purpose of the Site

This website is designed to educate and inspire by providing detailed information about Bolivia's native flowers. It includes their cultural significance, uses, and ecological importance, making it a hub for enthusiasts and researchers.

Visitor Scenarios

  • What is the national flower of Bolivia?
  • What do Bolivian flowers symbolize in local traditions?
  • How can I use Bolivian flowers in traditional remedies?

Color Schema

The site uses a warm, inviting color palette inspired by nature:

  • #fad168 (Yellow)
  • #805f04 (Brown)
  • #ebc90a (Gold)

Typography

The site employs two main fonts:

  • Poppins: Used for headings to give a clean and modern look.
  • Open Sans: Used for body text for readability and a professional appearance.
